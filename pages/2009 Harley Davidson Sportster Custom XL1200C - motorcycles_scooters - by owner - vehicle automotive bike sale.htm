For sale:
2009 Harley Davidson Sportster Custom XL1200C

This bike is in fantastic shape and has always been covered throughout its life. I am the second owner and am looking for a good home for this smooth, powerful motorcycle. The Sportster Custom is made to go the distance with a larger, 4.5 gallon fuel tank and forward controls for an extra comfortable ride. Bike has Screamin' Eagle slip-on mufflers and ignition-sounds cherry! Fuel injection means you don't have to worry about setting up carbs for altitude or for storage-a huge time saver! The bike has plenty of power and can cruise all day, backroads or interstate.

This bike comes with quick-release windshield, crash bars, cover, two keys, and a disc brake lock to help keep it secure. The bike also has a smart security system which requires a fob on your keychain to start-always a plus in Albuquerque!

Tires are in good shape and it's only been 500 or so miles since I changed the oil in both the engine and transmission. Brakes were also recently bled (added a front stainless steel brake line) and grips replaced with Biltwell Renegade Grips (and Biltwell throttle tube). Additionally, there is an LED headlight bulb installed which really brightened things up from the stock headlight-if you want the stock headlight bulb, it's yours.

Not really looking for trades but offers never hurt anyone. I work the typical Monday through Friday schedule but can always respond to text or email.

Asking $4250
